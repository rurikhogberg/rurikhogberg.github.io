RURIK Awarded the CSC Scholarship Pursuing Studies in Chinese Language

In August 2024, RURIK was invited to attend the Chinese Embassy in Stockholm’s Farewell Reception for Swedish Students Studying in China. The event gathered nearly 50 participants, including Chinese Government Scholarship recipients, exchange students, and alumni preparing to begin or continue their studies in China.

The reception emphasized the importance of educational exchange in strengthening bilateral ties.

RURIK’s participation in the event highlighted his continued engagement in international exchange and his commitment to fostering cross-cultural dialogue. Beyond its ceremonial significance, RURIK’s participation in the event marked an important chapter in his international academic journey. Having background in Business, Computer Science and Interaction Design, with, now, a deepening focus on Mandarin language studies at Fudan University, RURIK embodies the spirit of curiosity, adaptability, and bridge-building that cross-cultural exchanges are designed to foster.

His commitment to understanding China from within—not only through textbooks but through lived experience—aligns directly with the broader goals of the creating meaning full connection and bridging communities. By joining a new generation of globally-minded students eager to contribute to dialogue, innovation, and shared progress, RURIK continues to position himself at the intersection of technology, creativity and cross-cultural exchange—an intersection increasingly vital in our interconnected world.
